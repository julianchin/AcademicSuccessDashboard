About AcademicSuccessDashboard

AcademicSuccessDashboard is a web application that allows students and their advisors to monitor the status of students' academic status through a web interface. AcademicSuccessDashboard's mission is to help students and advisors monitor academic success by offering information about the current state of academic status and providing alert of potential failure in academic performance.
